Question 1 - Quantitative Analysis

D contains \(6.30\,g\,dm^{-3}\) of \(H_2C_2O_4\cdot2H_2O\). E contains \(3.10\,g\,dm^{-3}\) of \(KMnO_4\).

  1. Calculate the concentration of D in \(mol\,dm^{-3}\). [\(H_2C_2O_4\cdot2H_2O=126\)]
  2. Calculate the theoretical titre of E required for \(25.0\,cm^3\) of D.
  3. State why no external indicator is needed.
  4. State two errors that would make the titre too high.

Question 2 - Qualitative Analysis

F is tested with water, iodine, NaOH, NH3, BaCl2, Pb(NO3)2, HCl, HNO3 and zinc dust.

  1. Prepare a full observation and inference table.
  2. Write equations for the formation of \(Cu(OH)_2\), \(BaSO_4\), and displacement of copper by zinc.
  3. Explain why the residue and filtrate must be tested separately.

Question 3 - Apparatus and Theory

  1. Complete a table of apparatus and uses: pipette, clay triangle, crucible, bomb calorimeter.
  2. Arrange \(PbCl_2\), \(PbSO_4\), \(Pb(NO_3)_2\) in increasing order of solubility.
  3. State the effect on litmus of \(KNO_3(aq)\), \(K_2CO_3(aq)\), and \(Al_2(SO_4)_3(aq)\).
  4. Draw a labelled filtration setup.

Best A1 Solution

Question 1

\[C_D=\frac{6.30}{126}=0.0500\,mol\,dm^{-3}\]
\[C_E=\frac{3.10}{158}=0.0196\,mol\,dm^{-3}\]
\[n(C_2O_4^{2-})=\frac{0.0500\times25.0}{1000}=1.25\times10^{-3}\,mol\]
\[n(MnO_4^-)=\frac{2}{5}\times1.25\times10^{-3}=5.00\times10^{-4}\,mol\]
\[V_E=\frac{5.00\times10^{-4}\times1000}{0.0196}=25.5\,cm^3\]

Teacher: \(KMnO_4\) is purple and acts as a self-indicator. The endpoint is permanent pale pink.
